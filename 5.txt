How the request actually flows

Case A – NLB / Classic ELB with Service LoadBalancer:

Client → AWS LB (distributes by NodeIP:NodePort) → kube-proxy → PodIP:PodPort


Case B – ALB (Ingress with aws-load-balancer-controller):

Client → ALB (distributes by PodIP:PodPort directly) → Pod
