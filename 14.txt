+---------------------------------------------------------------+
|          Why Terraform + CI/CD Pipeline over eksctl?           |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+

 eksctl (Quick & Simple)                     Terraform + CI/CD (Scalable & Pro)
 -----------------------                     -----------------------------------
 - ✅ Easy to start                          - ✅ Infrastructure as Code (IaC)
 - ✅ Single command cluster                 - ✅ Version-controlled (GitOps)
 - ❌ Hard to manage HA / Prod               - ✅ Reproducible, Auditable
 - ❌ No state tracking                      - ✅ Manages State (S3 + DynamoDB)
 - ❌ Not great for automation               - ✅ CI/CD ready (CodePipeline/Jenkins/GitHub)
 - ❌ AWS-only, limited extensions           - ✅ Multi-cloud & modular

 Result:                                     Result:
 -------                                     -------
 Great for quick demos or POCs               Ideal for HA, Prod & Team workflows
                                             
+---------------------------------------------------------------+
|    Terraform + CI/CD = Repeatable, Reliable, Enterprise-ready  |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+
